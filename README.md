## Relay Services

An SRT relay sits between your encoder and your streaming platform, stabilising your connection automatically.

- [IRLHost](https://irlhost.gg) — Private SRT/SRTLA relay with automatic scene switching, chat commands, and real-time stream health monitoring. €12/month + VAT. Works with any SRT-compatible encoder (Moblin, IRL Pro, Belabox, OBS, etc.)
- [Belabox Cloud](https://belabox.net) — Bonding relay optimised for Belabox hardware encoders.
- [IRLServer](https://irlserver.com) — Global SRT relay endpoints.

**Comparison:**

| Service | Hosting | Price | Scene Switching | Affiliate |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| IRLHost | EU, US, Asia | €12/mo + VAT | Yes (built-in) | Yes (20%) |
| Belabox Cloud | Global | ~$10/mo | No | No |
| IRLServer | Global | Varies | No | No |

## Hardware Encoders

- [Belabox](https://belabox.net) — Open source software for Orange Pi / Jetson Nano. Excellent SRTLA bonding. Works with IRLHost as relay.
- [LiveU Solo](https://www.liveu.tv/products/solo) — Professional cellular bonding encoder.
- [GoPro](https://gopro.com) — Popular action camera with direct streaming support.

## Scene Switching
